Department Workflow

How the Consulting Department interacts with Minerva to bring projects to life

Our streamlined process ensures effective communication between the Consulting Department and Minerva mentors, resulting in successful project implementations for our scholars.

1

Ideation Request

Consulting Department submits a project request with initial requirements and goals.

2

Brainstorming & Approval

Minerva team evaluates feasibility, refines scope, and provides initial approval.

3

Pre-phase Documentation

Detailed project plan, technical requirements, and resource allocation documents are prepared.

4

Mentor Allocation

Specialized mentors are assigned based on project requirements and scholar needs.

5

Project Development

Scholars work with mentors to develop the project through an iterative process.

6

Delivery & Showcase

Completed projects are presented to stakeholders and added to the Minerva portfolio.
